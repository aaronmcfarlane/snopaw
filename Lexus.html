Lexus “Lexi”

 

Lexi is our beautiful and elegant foundation girl.  She is white with one black ear.  She was a gift from Joyce Ayotte , her Plattsburgh breeder and co-owner.  She came to us in 2002 and quickly established herself as our "Princess".  Lexi has a warm and cuddly personality and although she remembers and fusses over Joyce when she comes to visit, she is now also very attached to Deborah.  Lexi has had two big and beautiful litters (see Litters 2005 and 2007) for a total of 17 puppies.  We thought that that was quite enough and she is now spayed and seems to be enjoying her retirement.  Murdock was thrilled to be able to show Lexi and we were able to finish her Canadian championship which had been started before she came to us.  With her three puppies that are Canadian champions, she has her ROM  (Register of Merit).

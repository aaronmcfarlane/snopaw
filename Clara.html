Clara

 

Clara is a puppy from Lexi's composer litter of 2004. She is our sparkly, little, show girl.  What she lacks in size,  she makes up for in personality!  As soon as she had recovered from an early injury, she quickly earned her championship, beating much more mature and full-coated girls with her "look at me" attitude.  Her favourite things to do - jump on the dining room table from a standing position to hopefully find the butter dish - or - jump into Murdock's arms with a twist onto her back fora tummy rub!  She is Daddy's girl!  Since her Mother, Lexi, was spayed, Clara has taken over as top girl in the house.

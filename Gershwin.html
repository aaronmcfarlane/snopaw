Gershwin

 

Gershwin is Clara and Puccini's brother, a puppy from the Composer litter.  He is a handsome boy with a beautiful fall of head hair, large, soulful, dark eyes, and he has done well in the show ring with limited showing. But it's not really his style.  He prefers to sit at home, sit beside you on the couch and bump his nose under your arm until you pat him.  Gershwin loves to bark at his half-sister Cassi until she relents and plays with him.  His favourite place is in a dog bed with his toy - anything that still squeaks.  Whenever anyone comes home, he runs to find his stuffed duck, then returns to greet the person, squeaking madly the whole time. Gershwin always lets you know that he adores you.

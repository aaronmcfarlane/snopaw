Cassiopeia “Cassi”

 

Cassi is from Lexi's Nightstar litter of 2007.  She is our youngest TT and is still learning her manners.   (Maybe it is her cute black and white sidedness!)  The morning can't start until she has jumped in our lap and gone "nose to nose" with us.  She just smells and wags and then gets down but she is obviously checking us out. So far, I am pleased to say that we seem to pass!  She is our three year old grand-daughter's favourite because she is always playful and ready for a game. They run and chase and cuddle most of the time. Cassi has some points towards her Championship and she and Murdock are hoping to finish soon.

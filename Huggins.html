Huggins

 

Huggins is our first TT.  He is a beautiful chocolate neutered boy.  We were chosen as his family in 2001 by his breeder, Penny White who probably didn't realize just how many questions she would have to answer when she picked us!  Penny and Huggins have taught us much about TTs and the dog world.  Huggins even has two points towards his altered championship!  He was our first show dog!


Huggins is an independent TT who loves to yodel-talk to tell us when the dog dish or his stomach is empty! He seeks out the warmest spots - under the wood stove in the winter or squished in beside you in the arm chair. As a result of several years of obedience training when he was a puppy, Huggins still does a mean sit in front after a recall and a return to heel position, although, in true TT fashion, he was never able to do these commands during formal tests and thus, has no obedience letters after his name.  Huggins has earned his place in our home as "Uncle Huggins" because he loves to play with the newborn puppies as soon as the dam allows it.

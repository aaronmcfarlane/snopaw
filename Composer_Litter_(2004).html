Snopaw’s Bartok Bear

My name is Mary Shepardson and I live in Kentucky with my family of four sisters, a Mom and Dad and three dogs, a cat and a bunny! My dogs’ name is Bartok and he is now a four-year-old Tibetan Terrier. He plays a lot with the other dogs who are two Golden Retrievers that are named Sarah and Angel. They are both three
years old. They all like to go outside and play with each other. They will chase each other around for hours or run up and down the fence looking for other dogs to bark at. They are all indoor pets and love attention! Bartok sleeps with me in my room. He even has his own little spot at the end of the bed where he sleeps on a large stuffed bunny. My Dad eve
n named the bunny “Tougher Bunny” because Bartok likes to bark and we call him “tougher-than-most”. The other dogs sleep in my sisters’ rooms and Angel will even go into the room she sleeps in at a certain time at night like clockwork. My dog also loves to walk. My Dad will take them for a walk and when he gets the leashes out all the dogs run to him knowing that it is time for a walk. They also love to spend a lot of time with us! Since we are homeschooled during the school day they will spend a lot of time under our desks laying on our feet! Though most of all I love spending time with them! They love to be pet and they are always there with all of us!!!
